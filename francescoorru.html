We are proud to announce the sinister union of digital sculptor Francesco Orrù and Archivillain Games!

Francesco is a professional digital sculptor and freelance 3D artist based in London working on 3D printing, wargaming miniatures, collectibles and games. He has worked as a freelance digital sculptor at NECA on Dungeons & Dragons, Pathfinder and Heroclix to name a few. Francesco is characterized by perseverance and passion for CGI. A creative and resourceful individual, who is extremely dedicated to digital sculpting and 3D printing lover. More work from Francesco can be found on his website: http://4theswarm.co.uk.

P.S. We were kidding about the sinister part. Francesco is a good guy. Francesco. Not Archvillain, we‘re plain evil.
