About us

Our Dream

Above all, we are gamers. We believe that roleplaying games shaped much of who we are today. Our goal is to provide your gaming table with elements that treat Game Masters and characters like the center of the game that they are. We draw upon the classic and deliver immersion with innovative methods. We aim at building upon your stories, because every table has a story to tell.
